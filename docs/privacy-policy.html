Privacy Policy

Badger State Bite Book
Last updated: April 14, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Badger State Bite Book ("the App") handles your information. We take your privacy seriously and have designed the App to collect as little data as possible.

Information We Collect

  • Location — The App requests access to your device's location to show nearby fishing spots and provide local weather conditions. Location data is used only on-device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Journal Entries & Saved Spots — Any fishing journal entries or secret spots you save are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.
  • Weather Data — The App requests weather information from a third-party weather service using your approximate location. Please refer to that service's privacy policy for details on how they handle location data.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not create or require user accounts.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not transmit your journal entries, saved spots, or fishing records off your device.

Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — Used to show your position relative to fishing spots and to fetch local weather. You may deny this permission; some features will be limited.
  • INTERNET — Used to fetch weather data and fishing condition information from public sources.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
